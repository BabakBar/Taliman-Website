### 2.3 Processes Section

#### Cold Heading Quality (CHQ) Process
**Technical Overview:**
"Cold Heading Quality wire rod processing involves precise metallurgical treatment to optimize the material properties for cold forging operations. Our process ensures optimal ductility, uniform microstructure, and consistent mechanical properties essential for automotive fastener production."

#### Process Steps
1. **Material Inspection**: Incoming wire rod quality verification
2. **Bell-type Annealing**: Controlled atmosphere heat treatment
3. **Spheroidizing Treatment**: Microstructure optimization
4. **Precision Drawing**: Dimensional accuracy achievement
5. **Final Quality Control**: Comprehensive testing and certification

#### Applications
- Automotive bolts and screws
- Engine components
- Chassis fasteners
- High-strength fasteners
- Custom cold forged parts
